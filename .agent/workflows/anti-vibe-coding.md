# Anti-Vibe-Coding Design Standards

## The Problem

Many AI-generated designs fall into "vibe coding" aesthetics - recognizable patterns that signal amateurish, AI-generated work. This kills credibility for enterprise and professional sites.

## BANNED: Vibe-Coded Design Signals

### Colors to AVOID
- ❌ **Purple gradients** (#8B5CF6, #A855F7, etc.) - THE #1 tell-tale sign
- ❌ **Purple-to-pink gradients** - Screams "AI birthday party"
- ❌ **Neon purple/violet** - Overused in AI marketing
- ❌ **Pink accents** (#EC4899, #F472B6)
- ❌ **Rainbow gradients**
- ❌ **Generic blue-to-purple combos**

### Patterns to AVOID
- ❌ Magical sparkles or star icons
- ❌ Generic "AI brain" or neural network graphics
- ❌ Floating orbs with purple glow
- ❌ Glassmorphism with purple tints
- ❌ "Futuristic" neon outlines
- ❌ Gradient text with purple hues

## APPROVED: Enterprise/Premium Aesthetics

### Color Palettes

**Option 1: Deep Blue Tech (Recommended)**
- Primary: Electric Blue (#3B82F6)
- Secondary: Cyan (#06B6D4)
- Background: Pure Black (#000000) or Near-Black (#050505)
- Text: White (#FFFFFF), Gray (#9CA3AF)
- Accent: Subtle teal or green

**Option 2: Monochrome Professional**
- Primary: White (#FFFFFF)
- Secondary: Cool Gray (#6B7280)
- Background: Black (#000000)
- Accent: Single color (blue or green only)

**Option 3: Warm Enterprise (Finance/Legal)**
- Primary: Gold/Amber (#F59E0B) - SPARINGLY
- Secondary: White
- Background: Dark charcoal (#1F2937)

### Typography
- Clean sans-serif: Inter, Outfit, SF Pro, Helvetica
- High contrast (white on black)
- Generous letter-spacing for headlines
- NO gradient text unless single-color blue

### Imagery
- Abstract geometric shapes (subtle)
- Architectural/structural patterns
- Minimal iconography
- NO AI-themed stock graphics

## Social Media / OG Images

**DO:**
- Pure black or very dark background
- White or light gray text
- Single accent color (blue/cyan only)
- Clean, minimal layout
- Professional typography

**DON'T:**
- Purple/pink backgrounds
- Gradient backgrounds
- Busy neural network graphics
- Sparkles, stars, or magic effects
- Multiple bright colors
